# SEO Audit — 2026-07-20

## Executive summary

First baseline audit for the PCM IT Services website. The site is live with strong service-page content and a **working sitemap** (200 OK — prior 500 error appears resolved), but **all pages serve identical server-side HTML** with the generic title `Hostinger Horizons` and no meta description, canonical, or schema in the initial response. PCM does not rank in the top 10 for any of the nine tracked keywords; established MSPs (BeckTek, Adaptive Office Solutions, Annexus Technologies) dominate with dedicated location landing pages, FAQs, and review signals. Immediate priority: fix per-page SEO settings in Hostinger so titles/descriptions render in HTML, then publish Moncton + Dieppe location pages.

## Critical issues

1. **Server-side title is `Hostinger Horizons` on every URL** — Raw HTML (6344 bytes, identical for all routes) contains `<title>Hostinger Horizons</title>` with no `<meta name="description">`, no canonical, and no JSON-LD. Social crawlers, Bing, and Google’s initial crawl see a generic SPA shell. **Impact:** Suppresses rankings for all target keywords; weak link-preview snippets.

2. **No LocalBusiness / Organization schema** — Competitors expose structured data; PCM has zero `application/ld+json` in source HTML. **Impact:** Weak local pack eligibility and rich-result signals.

3. **Internal/utility pages in sitemap (17 URLs)** — `quote-builder`, `client-onboarding`, `ai-agent-requirements`, `it-service-assessment`, and `ai-readiness-assessment` are indexed alongside marketing pages. **Impact:** Crawl budget dilution; thin/utility URLs compete with service pages.

- **Fix (Hostinger):** For **each page** → open in Website Builder → **Page Settings** (gear icon) → **SEO Settings** → enter custom **Page title** and **Meta description** → Save → **Publish** site. Repeat for all 9 public marketing pages. After publish, verify with `browser View Page Source on the homepage` — title must NOT say “Hostinger Horizons”.

- **Fix (Hostinger):** Page Settings → **Custom code** (or site-wide Head HTML) on **Contact** and **Homepage** → paste JSON-LD below → Publish.

```json
  "@type": "LocalBusiness",
  "name": "PCM IT Services",
  "url": "<your-site-root-url>",

  "email": "support@<your-domain>",

  "priceRange": "$$"
}
```
